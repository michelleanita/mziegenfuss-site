CutieDo's: A To Do App

CutieDo's is a To Do App designed for the Lisa Frank lover who was in middle school in the 90's. An overload of cute with lots of silly characters, this app is super simple and easy for everyone to use. The interface is highly customizable and users may choose from various background themes, list icons, fonts, and color palettes.

Color Mini Site

The Color Mini Site is an exploration into color schemes on the web. You will find six combinations, including analogous, monochromatic, triad, tetrad, and split complementary palettes. Definitions are provided to educate those unfamiliar with the vocabulary. Utilizing the color wheel and employing harmonies is a tried and true method of achieving a succesful color palette in your design.
