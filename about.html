About Me

Hello! My name is Michelle and I am a digital designer living in Alexandria, Virginia. I have a degree in Strategic Communication from Temple University and am currently studying both Graphic and Interactive Design at Northern Virginia Community College. I also enjoy backpacking, fishing, photography, dancing, live music, cooking and baking.

Design Style

I like to take a step back and consider the bigger picture. I ask lots of questions and do not skimp on research in order to find the best solutions. I act as both a creative and strategic resource who assists with marketing and brand strategy. My work focuses on web design and digital marketing, branding and identity development, and print and material design.
